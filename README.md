# Sales Performance & Customer Insights Analysis

This project demonstrates an end-to-end data analytics workflow focused on transforming raw transactional data into actionable business insights. The emphasis is on analytical reasoning, metric definition, and decision support rather than tool usage alone.

---

## Business Context
The project is based on a simulated retail dataset containing sales transactions, customer information, product details, and regional data. The objective is to help business stakeholders understand performance drivers, identify growth opportunities, and improve customer retention.

---

## Objectives
- Evaluate overall sales and profitability trends across regions and time
- Identify top-performing and underperforming products
- Analyze customer value, purchase behavior, and retention patterns
- Translate analytical findings into intuitive dashboards for decision-making

---

## Analytical Approach
1. **Data Modeling**
   - Structured transactional data into analytical tables
   - Created SQL views to standardize key business metrics

2. **Metric Definition**
   - Revenue calculated with discounts applied
   - Profit derived from unit price and cost per unit
   - Customer value measured through total revenue and order frequency
   - Retention assessed using active customers over time

3. **Insight Development**
   - Explored trends, patterns, and anomalies in sales performance
   - Compared customer segments based on purchasing behavior
   - Identified concentration risks and high-impact products

---

## Tools Used
- **SQL Server**: Data modeling, joins, aggregations, and analytical views
- **Tableau**: Interactive dashboards and visual storytelling
